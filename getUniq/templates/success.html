Be Patient While Your U-M Google Account Is Set Up

It could take as long as 24-48 hours for your new Google at U-M account to be set up. If you aren't able to log in right away, please wait a couple of hours and try again.

  • For most people at U-M, a U-M Google account provides access to a full suite of collaboration tools, including Google Mail, Calendar, Drive, Google+, and more. U-M Google Mail and Drive include unlimited storage.
  • Incoming medical students, Michigan Medicine employees, and others who must comply with various federal regulations that place restrictions on how sensitive regulated data is handled receive a U-M Google account that does not include Mail and Calendar. They use an email and calendar service provided by Michigan Medicine instead.
  • UM-Flint campus. Those on the Flint campus receive a U-M Google account that does not include Mail and Calendar. They use an email and calendar service provided by Flint Information Technology Services instead.
